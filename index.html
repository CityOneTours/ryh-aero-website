Power Solutions

Aircraft Batteries

Critical power for aircraft emergency systems — extremely reliable, low on maintenance, and high on efficiency across all aviation platforms.

Batteries are a critical component onboard to power aircraft emergency systems safely and effectively. As the last line of defence in the emergency chain, aviation demands stored power solutions that are extremely reliable, low on maintenance, and high on efficiency.

We supply aviation batteries across three proven technologies, finding applications in commercial and defence aircraft, helicopters, turboprops, business jets, and UAVs — for new airframes or as aftermarket replacements.

Our Story

Aviation is More
Than a Business

For over 15 years, RYH Aero has been connecting the world through dependable aviation services — from charter solutions to the smallest spare part that keeps an aircraft flying.

Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Driven by Excellence

<

RYH Aero was founded with a single mission: to provide aviation operators with a reliable, one-stop partner for all their operational needs. Through our global presence and commitment to excellence, we serve clients across Asia, the Middle East, Africa, and beyond, delivering comprehensive aviation solutions tailored to the needs of modern operators.

Our team combines decades of aviation industry expertise with an unwavering commitment to safety, compliance, and customer satisfaction. We understand that in aviation, delays cost lives and livelihoods — which is why we operate 24/7 with urgency baked into every process.

From sourcing a critical spare part for an AOG aircraft to coordinating complex multi-leg cargo charters, we bring the same level of dedication to every client engagement, regardless of scale.
